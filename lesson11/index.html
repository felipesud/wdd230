meet carlsbad

More Than Just A City

Carlsbad is a coastal city located in the North County region of San Diego County, California, the city has a total area of 39.1 square miles (101 km2) of which 37.7 square miles (98 km2) is land and 1.4 square miles (3.6 km2) are water (3.55%), most of which are found within three miles lakes and one lake.
