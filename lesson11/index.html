meet carlsbad

Location

Carlsbad is a coastal city located in the North County region of San Diego County, California, the city has a total area of 39.1 square miles (101 km2) of which 37.7 square miles (98 km2) is land and 1.4 square miles (3.6 km2) are water (3.55%), most of which are found within three miles lakes and one lake.

Statistics

According to the United States Census Bureau, the city's estimated population in 2021 was 115,302.
The population under 18 is 23.2%, above 65 years is 16.5%, and 50.7% of the total population are women.
Median household income is $112,933 per year.

Attractions

Carlsbad has beautiful beaches, the LEGOLAND theme park, the Carlsbad Aquarium, the Flower Fields of Carlsbad Ranch, which has 50 acres of spectacular flowers in bloom every spring, the Premium Outlets, for excellent shopping and has a variety of options for restaurants and bars for a night out.
