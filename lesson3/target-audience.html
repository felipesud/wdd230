Target Audience

Tech Recruiters/Headhunters looking for a web developer and small/medium business entrepreneurs looking for a website or a web system.

Two Personas

Persona 1: A clothes store owner looking for build an e-commerce to sell his products

Persona 2: A Real Estate company that haven't website to show their services.

Five Listed Scenarios

Scenario 1:Ana is a headhunter from a software company, found my profile on social media and entered into my portfolio to see what I know to do with coding

Scenario 2:Kevin is a civil engineer and entered my portfolio looking for a web developer who can develop his portfolio.

Scenario 3:John is a Real Estate entrepreneur, but his company does have not a website yet. He found me on social media and look at my site seeking someone who can help him to be more visible on the internet.

Scenario 4:I applied for a new job, and the HR analysts looked at my site to see my experience and decide if I'm able to be hired for that position.

Scenario 5:Russell sells soccer shirts on Instagram but sees that he needs to be more professional and be able to attend to customers from the whole country. He found my website and call me asking about developing e-commerce.
